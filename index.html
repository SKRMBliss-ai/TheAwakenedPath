The Awakened Path: Core Philosophy & Practice

The Awakened Path is a specialized spiritual application and journey designed by Awakened Path Studio. It is based exclusively on the modern spiritual teachings of Michael A. Singer (The Untethered Soul) and Eckhart Tolle (The Power of Now).

Core Practice: Witness Consciousness

The primary practice is the art of Witnessing — becoming the objective observer of the mind, thoughts, and emotions. This is not traditional meditation or "Light & Sound" meditation; it is the practice of staying behind the noise of the mind.

  • Relax and Release: The Michael Singer method of letting go of inner disturbances (Samskaras) as they arise.
  • Presence: The Eckhart Tolle method of anchoring attention in the Now to dissolve the egoic mind.
  • Objective Observation: Identifying as the consciousness that hears the thoughts, rather than the thoughts themselves.

Project Identity

This project is independent and focused on secular, experiential spiritual growth through the frameworks of 'The Untethered Soul' and 'The Power of Now'. It is hosted at skrmblissai.in/awakenedpath as part of the Awakened Path digital ecosystem.
