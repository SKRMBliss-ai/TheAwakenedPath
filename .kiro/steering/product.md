# Product: Mind Gym

Mind Gym is a gamified mindfulness and presence-intelligence app grounded exclusively in the teachings of **Michael A. Singer** (*The Untethered Soul*) and **Eckhart Tolle** (*The Power of Now*). The goal is to guide users from conceptual thinking into Witness Consciousness — the ability to observe the mind objectively rather than being swept along by it.

## Core Features

- **Daily Journey / Dashboard** — ambient entry point with a daily recommended practice and inspirational quote. Gamification metrics (XP, streaks) are de-emphasized on the home screen; the focus is the immediate next action.
- **Practice Library** — Quick Shifts (< 1 min resets), Deep Dives (multi-step guided sessions), Situational Practices, and a rotating Weekly Assignment.
- **Presence Intelligence** — AI-guided (Gemini) tools: Body Truth Test, Witness & Release, Grounding Anchor, Emotion Analysis, and Power of Now chapter teachings.
- **Live Group Meditation Room** — WebRTC-based silent meditation sessions with instructor media controls, participant gallery, and ambient audio chimes.
- **Sacred Soundscapes (Music Hub)** — premium ambient audio marketplace with flip-card UI, Razorpay per-track purchase, and secure Firebase Storage streaming.
- **Courses Hub** — structured wisdom courses (Wisdom Untethered, Power of Now chapters) with chapter-level progress tracking.
- **Journaling & Release** — guided journal forms with body map, Witness & Release tool, and AI-generated daily meditation.
- **Gamification** — XP, levels, streaks, Witness/Presence/Zen Points, energy alignment bar, achievements (16 across 4 categories), and a token economy for feature access.
- **Token / Trial System** — new users get 300 tokens (7-day trial); premium features deduct tokens per use; Razorpay payment wall when exhausted.

## Target Audience

Primary users are **40+ years old** seeking secular, experiential mastery of presence. Simplicity, clarity, large touch targets, and mobile/iPad optimization are non-negotiable.

## Tone of Voice

All UI text must be **spiritual, calming, encouraging, and concise**. Use words like "Journey," "Witness," "Guidance," "Flow," "Presence." Avoid technical jargon, bureaucratic labels, or cluttering motivational taglines.
