The Awakened Path Philosophy

The Awakened Path is an advanced spiritual journey focused on the teachings of Michael A. Singer and Eckhart Tolle.

  • Objective: Spiritual liberation through witness consciousness.
  • Method: Relaxing and releasing (The Untethered Soul) and mastery of the Present Moment (The Power of Now).
  • Practices: Letting go of the inner roommate, objective observation, and non-resistance.
